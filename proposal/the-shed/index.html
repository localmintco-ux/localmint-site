02 — Your Custom VIP Membership Offer

Built around what your regulars already order.

Every element below was chosen specifically for The Shed's menu, format, and guest behavior. Not a template.

The Hook · Complimentary every visit
Shed Fries + Shed Sauce, on us.
Menu price $8.95
Food cost ~$2.00

Every member, every visit, gets one order of Shed Fries with Shed Sauce delivered to the table. One per table, comped automatically.

Why this works for The Shed

Shed Fries are already a top-five item by attach rate, the Shed Sauce is part of the brand identity (it shows up on the burgers, the Reuben, and as a default mention from servers), and at a ~$2 food cost against an $8.95 menu price you're handing the guest an item that feels like it costs four times what it costs you. More importantly: it creates a behavioral trigger. Members start saying "let's grab Shed fries" the way people say "let's grab a coffee." That's the language pattern we're paying for.

Discount

15% off the entire menu, every visit.

Applies to food, cocktails, beer, and wine — including the Espresso Martini, the Shed Burger, and the 12oz Sirloin. The discount is small enough to protect your margin and big enough to feel real on a $70 check.

Exclusive Perk · 01

Lobster Bowl & seasonals — year-round access. (optional)

Members get the Lobster Bowl and other seasonal items added to a private members-only menu, available year-round (when supply allows). This is the perk regulars will actually talk about. Costs you nothing when it's in season anyway.

Exclusive Perk · 02

Priority seating on busy nights. (optional)

Members text ahead and get bumped to the front of the wait list. No reservation system overhaul required — just a simple status flag in your existing FOH flow. Full reservations (Resy/OpenTable integration) available as a Phase 2 upgrade.

What members will see at the table

A faithful mockup of The Shed's existing menu — same paper, same layout, same hand-drawn flourishes — with member pricing layered next to each item, the Shed Fries highlighted as a free hook item, and the Lobster Bowl badged as a year-round members-only exclusive.
