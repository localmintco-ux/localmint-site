The Problem

Your best customers are worth a fortune. You're leaving it on the table.

Unpredictable Revenue

You never know if next Tuesday will be a $2,000 day or an $800 day. Your income depends entirely on who walks through the door.

No Customer Lock-In

Your regulars love you, but there's nothing financially tying them to your restaurant. One bad experience or a new spot opens and they drift.

Lower Business Valuation
